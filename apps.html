Mobile Apps

iPhone or Android?

Apps to make make your life a little easier.

I've developed Android and iOS apps in Android Studio using Java, Dart, and Flutter.

My Apps.

Random Student Picker

Enter names into a list and tap the roll button to randomly select a name. Don't like the order of the list? Choose to randomize, alphabetize, or clear the list altogether!

Enhanced Dice Roller

Choose to roll between 1 and 5 dice for various lengths of time. Customize your dice by changing the color, or by changing the number of sides of the dice to 4, 6, or 10!

Pizza Party

Can't figure out how many pizzas to order for your party? The Pizza Party App takes into account how many people are at your party and how hungry they are and recommends the number pizzas you should buy!

Bounce Surface

Watch the ball bounce back and forth around the screen. Soothe your OCD by waiting for it to perfectly hit a corner. If you don't like the size, color, speed, or direction of the ball, simply tap it to spawn a different one!
